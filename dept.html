Allergy and Immunology

Cooper allergists-immunologists see patients of all ages, including infants, children, adults and senior citizens (geriatric population).

Anesthesiology

Anesthesiologists are perioperative physicians who provide medical care to patients before, during, and after surgical procedures. Preparation of patients for emergency surgery are mandatory, essential and critical skills that anesthesiologists have been trained to employ.

B

Bloodless Care Program

The Bloodless Care Program provides patients with an alternative when making health care choices and offers a wide range of medical services without the utilization of blood transfusions. We offer this alternative as a means of honoring the religious beliefs and medical preferences of our patients.

Brain Tumor Program

The Apollo Neurological Institute has established the Brain Tumor Treatment Center as a multidisciplinary treatment and research program.

C

Community Health

Within Apollo’s community health outreach, the goal is to promote healthy behaviors and preventative care in collaboration with community partners. Our staff provides health education programs, presentations and consultations in an effort to engage, empower and educate the community members we serve.

Critical Care Medicine

Critical Care Physicians care for seriously ill or injured patients while they are in the hospital. They work in cooperation with the patient's primary care physician and other medical or surgical specialists as needed.

D

Digestive Health Institute

The Cooper Digestive Health Institute’s physician experts provide the most advanced and comprehensive gastroenterology care in South Jersey. Offering the latest endoscopy and other non-surgical technologies, the Cooper Digestive Health Institute provides patients with expert care in a welcoming environment designed for maximum comfort.
